After years of building products for others, it felt right to finally build something for ourselves. The new Foxteck portfolio is live at [foxteck.com](https://www.foxteck.com) and catalogs the full breadth of Blake Fox's work — from co-founding AgTech startups and shipping holographic display hardware to leading AI platform development at IBM and building AAA game infrastructure at Sony Online Entertainment.

The site was built with [Astro](https://astro.build), Tailwind CSS, and a static-first architecture — fast, accessible, and deployable anywhere. Projects are authored in Markdown with a type-safe content collection schema, making it easy to add and maintain entries over time.

## What's Inside

- **20+ projects** spanning AI, hardware, games, medical, and enterprise
- Full project detail pages with media galleries and video embeds
- A personal timeline tracing the full career arc from 2000 to present
- Services offered through Foxteck LLC — from AI consulting to product leadership

More updates coming as new projects are completed and published. Stay tuned.
