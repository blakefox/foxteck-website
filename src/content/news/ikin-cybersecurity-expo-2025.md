IKIN announced that Blake Fox, Vice President of Engineering, will speak at the **Enterprise Cybersecurity Expo** (February 11–13, 2025) at the Greater Ft. Lauderdale/Broward County Convention Center.

His session — **"Defending Tomorrow: The Convergence of Cybersecurity and Artificial Intelligence"** — is scheduled for Thursday, February 13 at 12:00 p.m. The talk will address strategies for protecting data and intellectual property in an increasingly AI-driven landscape, where threats range from direct attacks to the misappropriation of brand assets and other forms of digital intellectual property.

> "Cyber threats come in many forms, including attacks, theft, and the misappropriation of business assets, such as brand images and other forms of intellectual property."

Blake brings perspectives from over two decades of hardware and software product development across IBM Watson, Electronic Arts, Disney, and Nickelodeon — giving him a uniquely cross-industry lens on where AI amplifies both the attack surface and the defensive toolkit.
