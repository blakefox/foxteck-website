IKIN announced that CEO Joe Ward and Vice President of Engineering Blake Fox will speak at **ITEXPO** and its co-located **Enterprise Metaverse and Future of Work Expo**, held February 13–15, 2024 at the Greater Ft. Lauderdale/Broward County Convention Center.

Blake Fox is scheduled for two sessions on Tuesday, February 13:

- **"Brave New World – When Virtual Becomes Reality"** (11:00 a.m.) — exploring practical VR, AR, and MR use cases for enterprise and consumer applications
- **"Tech to Enhance the Metaverse for Your Business"** (3:00 p.m.) — a panel discussion on the technologies enabling real-world metaverse deployments

Joe Ward will separately present **"Reimagining Business in the Metaverse"** (2:00 p.m.), discussing emerging opportunities for enterprises navigating the spatial computing transition.

The sessions reflect IKIN's position at the intersection of immersive display hardware and the software infrastructure required to make holographic and AR experiences commercially viable — without the headgear that has historically limited consumer adoption.
