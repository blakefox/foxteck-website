Blake Fox, Vice President of Engineering at IKIN, joined host Priya Dialani on the **Tech Trends Podcast** to explore how artificial intelligence is fundamentally changing the way video is compressed, transmitted, and displayed.

The conversation centers on IKIN's mission to deliver immersive experiences without requiring headgear — a constraint that drove deep innovation in both hardware and software. Blake discusses **Asimov**, IKIN's AI-powered toolset that applies generative AI and computer vision to enhance video quality and augment reality experiences across consumer and enterprise platforms.

> "AI-driven video regeneration is transforming video technology, bridging the gap between quality and compression, and paving the way for exceptional digital experiences."

The episode covers how traditional compression algorithms sacrifice visual fidelity at the expense of bandwidth, and why IKIN's diffusion-based approach regenerates video at the destination rather than simply discarding data — producing output that is perceptually nearly identical to the original at a fraction of the transmission cost.
