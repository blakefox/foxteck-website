IKIN announced the appointment of Blake Fox as Vice President of Engineering. In this role, Fox leads IKIN's hardware and software engineering teams in the development and delivery of holographic display solutions — technology that delivers immersive experiences without requiring goggles or headgear.

> "Blake's years of product development and delivery experience, combined with his talent for leading engineering teams, make him an incredible fit for IKIN." — Taylor Scott, Chief Technology Officer

Fox joins IKIN from HIA Technologies, where he served as Vice President of Engineering and Product, leading development of immersive content platforms for AI-powered healthcare applications across 3D, VR, and AR environments. His broader background spans facial animation software, rural technology adoption initiatives, and work with IBM Watson on immersive supercomputer experiences.

The announcement coincides with two significant IKIN milestones: recognition by **Frost & Sullivan** as a global technology leader for holographic solutions, and the commercial release of **IKIN ARC™** — the world's first ambient-light holographic display requiring no goggles or headgear.
