Blake Fox and the Foxteck team are registered to exhibit at the **30th Annual Sandhills Ranch Expo** in Bassett, Nebraska — one of the region's premier gatherings for ranchers, farmers, and agricultural industry professionals.

The team will have a dedicated booth on **June 19th** showcasing **Livestalk**, Foxteck's computer vision platform for automated livestock inventory and cattle tracking. Attendees can stop by for live demos of the technology in action and speak directly with Blake and the team about how the platform can be adapted to fit the specific needs of their operation — whether that's a small family ranch or a large commercial cattle enterprise.

A core goal of the expo presence is listening. The Sandhills ranching community brings deep, practical knowledge of livestock management, and that real-world feedback directly shapes how Livestalk evolves.

If you're attending the expo, **make sure to stop by the Foxteck booth.** If you can't make it in person, visit [www.livestalk.org](https://www.livestalk.org) to learn more about the technology, sign up for a free trial, or get in touch with the team.
