Led IKIN's 3D scanning technology engagement with Estée Lauder — one of the world's leading beauty companies — delivering volumetric capture and rendering capabilities tailored for beauty tech applications.

The project explored IKIN's computer vision and volumetric scanning pipeline as a tool for product visualization, skin analysis, and immersive brand experiences. The collaboration demonstrated how IKIN's holographic and scanning technologies could translate from entertainment into high-value enterprise applications in the beauty and luxury goods space.
