A collection of R&D proof-of-concept demonstrations built to validate IKIN's holographic display pipeline and showcase its capabilities to potential partners, investors, and enterprise clients.

These demos — ranging from interactive 3D product visualization (wine bottle character, geometric objects) to real-time animation — served as the proving ground for the core perceptual and rendering systems that power both the RYZ and ARC platforms. Each POC stress-tested a different aspect of the holographic pipeline: rendering quality, interaction responsiveness, content delivery, and the perceptual tricks that make flat-screen holograms convincing to the eye.
